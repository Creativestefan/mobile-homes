# **Mobile Home Dealership Website Blueprint**

This document outlines the design style, demo inventory, page structure, and exact copywriting for a mobile home sales and financing website.

## **1\. Design Style & Brand Guidelines**

**The Vibe:** futuristicTrustworthy, Accessible, and Warm. The goal is to make homeownership feel attainable, regardless of a user's financial background, while maintaining a highly professional appearance for the lending/financing side of the business.

**Color Palette:**

* **Primary (Navy/Slate):** \#0F172A \- Represents trust, stability, and professionalism. Used for headers, footers, and primary text.  
* **Secondary (Emerald/Forest Green):** \#059669 \- Represents land, growth, and the idea of settling down. Used for badges, icons, and secondary buttons.  
* **Accent (Warm Amber/Gold):** \#D97706 \- Used sparingly for high-priority Call-To-Action (CTA) buttons (e.g., "Get Prequalified").  
* **Backgrounds:** Clean whites (\#FFFFFF) and soft grays (\#F8FAFC) to keep the inventory looking fresh and uncluttered.

**Typography:**

* **Primary Font:** Inter or Roboto (Clean, modern sans-serif).  
* **Usage:** Highly legible on mobile devices, paired with strong, heavy font weights for headings to convey stability.

**UI/UX Principles:**

* **Mobile-First:** Since many users will be browsing on their phones, buttons must be large, text easily readable, and image galleries swipeable.  
* **Trust Signals:** Prominently display phone numbers, physical addresses, and "Secure Application" badges.

## **3\. Page Structure & Copywriting**

### **Page 1: Home**

**Structure:** Hero Banner \-\> Featured Inventory \-\> Value Proposition \-\> Testimonials \-\> Final CTA \-\> Footer.

**Hero Section Copy:**

* **Eyebrow/Kicker:** Welcome Home  
* **Headline:** Your Path to Affordable Homeownership  
* **Subheadline:** Discover top-quality new and pre-owned manufactured homes, tiny homes, and modular living spaces. We provide full-service delivery, setup, and flexible financing.  
* **Primary CTA:** Browse Inventory  
* **Secondary CTA:** Get Prequalified

**Featured Inventory Copy:**

* **Heading:** Featured Homes  
* **Subheading:** Hand-picked models available for immediate delivery.

**Value Proposition Copy (3 Pillars):**

* **Section Heading:** More Than Just a Dealership  
* **Section Subheading:** We handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on moving in. From finding the perfect lot to securing your loan, we're with you every step.  
* **Pillar 1:** *Flexible Financing* \- We work with a network of lenders to offer competitive rates, seller financing, and lease-to-own programs for all credit scores.  
* **Pillar 2:** *Delivery & Setup* \- Our logistics team handles transport, blocking, leveling, and utility connections across the region.  
* **Pillar 3:** *We Buy Homes* \- Looking to upgrade or just need cash fast? We offer fair market value cash buyouts for your current mobile home.

**Testimonials Copy:**

* **Section Heading:** What Our Homeowners Say  
* **Review 1:** "We never thought we could afford a home, but the financing team made it happen. The setup was seamless, and we love our new space\!" — *Sarah & Mark T.*  
* **Review 2:** "They bought our old single-wide for cash and had us set up in a beautiful new modular home in record time. Incredible, stress-free service." — *David R.*  
* **Review 3:** "From browsing the inventory online to the final walkthrough, everything was transparent and easy. Highly recommend\!" — *Jessica M.*

**Final CTA Section Copy:**

* **Section Heading:** Ready to Step Into Your New Home?  
* **Section Subheading:** Don't wait. Browse our current inventory or see what financing programs you qualify for today.  
* **Primary Button:** Shop All Homes  
* **Secondary Button:** Get Prequalified Now

### **Page 2: Inventory (Shop Homes)**

**Structure:** Page Header \-\> Filter Bar \-\> Property Grid \-\> Property Detail Modal/Page.

**Header Copy:**

* **Headline:** Current Inventory  
* **Subheadline:** Browse our wide selection of ready-to-move homes. Use the filters below to find the perfect size for your family.

**Filter Categories:**

* All | Single Wide | Double Wide | Tiny Home | Triple Wide

**Card Details (What displays on each home):**

* Image, Title, Price, Estimated Monthly Payment (Price \* 0.008), Beds, Baths, Sqft, "View Details" button.

### **Page 3: Financing**

**Structure:** Two-column layout. Left side: Persuasive copy and programs. Right side: Lead capture form.

**Left Column Copy:**

* **Kicker:** Finance Your Dream  
* **Headline:** Homeownership is within your reach.  
* **Body:** We believe everyone deserves a place to call home. With over 20 years of experience, our finance team has built relationships with top national lenders and local credit unions to get you approved.  
* **Bullet 1:** *Chattel (Home-Only) Loans* \- Perfect if you are placing the home in a rented community or park.  
* **Bullet 2:** *Land & Home Packages* \- Bundle the cost of the home, the land, and the setup into one single monthly payment.  
* **Bullet 3:** *Bad Credit / No Credit Programs* \- Ask about our in-house seller financing and lease-to-own options. We work with all backgrounds.

**Right Column (Form Fields):**

* First Name, Last Name  
* Email Address  
* Phone Number  
* Estimated Credit Score (Dropdown: Excellent 720+, Good 680-719, Fair 640-679, Needs Work \<640, I don't know)  
* **Submit Button:** Submit Application Securely  
* **Disclaimer text:** By submitting, you agree to our terms. This will not affect your credit score.

### **Page 4: Sell Your Home**

**Structure:** High-contrast, dark-themed hero section blending into a simple lead-capture form.

**Copywriting:**

* **Headline:** Need to sell your mobile home? We buy them for cash.  
* **Body:** Skip the real estate agents, the repairs, and the waiting. We buy manufactured homes in any condition, handle the tear-down and transport, and put cash in your hands fast.  
* **Benefit 1:** *Fast Cash Offers* \- Get an evaluation and an offer within 48 hours.  
* **Benefit 2:** *We Handle Moving* \- Don't worry about logistics; our fleet handles removal from your lot.

**Form Fields:**

* Make / Builder (e.g., Clayton, Champion)  
* Year Built  
* Size Type (Dropdown: Single, Double, Modular, RV)  
* Current Location (City, State)  
* Your Name  
* Your Phone Number  
* **Submit Button:** Get My Cash Offer
